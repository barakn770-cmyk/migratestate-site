Privacy Policy

MS MigrateState  ·  Last updated June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MigrateState ("we", "us", "our"), operator of migratestate.com (the "Site"), collects, uses, and safeguards information when you visit the Site or subscribe to our newsletter. By using the Site, you agree to the practices described here.

Who we are

MigrateState publishes informational guides for Americans buying property and residency abroad. We are a publisher of educational content; we are not a law firm, tax-advisory firm, brokerage, or financial institution, and using the Site does not create any professional or advisory relationship.

Information we collect

We collect only what we need to run the Site and send the content you ask for:

  • Information you give us. When you subscribe to our newsletter or checklist, we collect the email address you submit. If you contact us, we receive whatever information you include in your message.
  • Information collected automatically. Like most websites, we collect standard technical data through analytics — such as your device and browser type, approximate location (derived from IP address), pages viewed, referring page, and time on site. This is collected via Google Analytics using cookies and similar technologies.

We do not ask for or intentionally collect sensitive personal information (such as government identifiers, financial account numbers, or passwords) through this Site, and you should never send such information to us by email.

How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • send the newsletter, the buyer's checklist, and related updates you requested;
  • understand how the Site is used so we can improve our content;
  • respond to your questions or messages; and
  • maintain the security and proper functioning of the Site, and comply with legal obligations.

Cookies and analytics

The Site uses cookies and similar technologies, primarily for Google Analytics, to measure traffic and usage. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings, and you can opt out of Google Analytics using Google's opt-out browser add-on. Disabling cookies may affect some Site features but will not prevent you from reading our guides.

Email and the newsletter

Our newsletter is managed through a third-party email service provider (currently MailerLite), which stores your email address and engagement data on our behalf so we can deliver messages. Every email includes an unsubscribe link, and you can opt out at any time. We do not sell your email address.

Affiliate links and partners

Some links on the Site are affiliate or referral links. If you click one and take an action (such as contacting a recommended professional or service), we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. Where you proceed to a partner's website or form, that partner's own privacy practices apply — we encourage you to review them.

Third parties we share data with

We do not sell your personal information. We share limited data only with service providers that help us operate the Site, under their respective terms, including: our analytics provider (Google Analytics), our email provider (MailerLite), and our hosting/content-delivery provider. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

Data retention

We keep newsletter subscriber data for as long as you remain subscribed or until you ask us to delete it. Analytics data is retained according to our analytics provider's standard retention settings.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under laws such as the EU/UK GDPR or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, and to opt out of certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below. You can unsubscribe from emails at any time via the link in any message.

International visitors

The Site is operated for a primarily US audience and our service providers may process data in the United States and other countries. By using the Site, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own.

Children's privacy

The Site is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be reflected on this page; your continued use of the Site after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data? Email us at privacy@migratestate.com.

This Privacy Policy is provided for general informational purposes and does not constitute legal advice.
