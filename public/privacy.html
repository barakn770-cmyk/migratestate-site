Privacy Policy

MS MigrateState  ·  Last updated June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MigrateState ("we", "us", "our"), operator of migratestate.com (the "Site"), collects, uses, and safeguards information when you visit the Site, subscribe to our newsletter, or submit our matching ("Get Started") form. By using the Site, you agree to the practices described here.

Who we are

MigrateState publishes informational guides for people buying property and residency abroad and, with your consent, connects you with vetted independent professionals. We are a publisher and a referral/matching service; we are not a law firm, tax-advisory firm, brokerage, or financial institution, and using the Site does not create any professional or advisory relationship with us.

Information we collect

We collect only what we need to run the Site, send the content you ask for, and match you with relevant professionals when you request it:

  • Information you give us. When you subscribe to our newsletter or checklist, we collect the email address you submit. When you complete our Get Started / matching form, we also collect the enquiry details you provide — such as your name, email, phone number, nationality, target country, budget, timeline, and financing preference. If you contact us, we receive whatever information you include in your message.
  • Information collected automatically. Like most websites, we collect standard technical data through analytics — such as your device and browser type, approximate location (derived from IP address), pages viewed, referring page, and time on site. This is collected via Google Analytics using cookies and similar technologies.

We do not ask for sensitive personal information (such as government identifiers, financial account numbers, or passwords) through this Site, and you should never send such information to us by email or through our forms.

How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • send the newsletter, the buyer's checklist, and related updates you requested;
  • match you with relevant vetted partner professionals when you submit the Get Started form and consent (see below);
  • understand how the Site is used so we can improve our content;
  • respond to your questions or messages; and
  • maintain the security and proper functioning of the Site, and comply with legal obligations.

Lead matching and sharing with partners

The Get Started form is a matching service. When you submit it and tick the consent box, you ask us to connect you with suitable professionals — and you agree that we may share the enquiry details you provided with one or more vetted partner professionals (such as lawyers, mortgage brokers, tax advisers, and licensed residency/Golden-Visa specialists) relevant to your request, so that they can contact you about it.

Important points about this sharing:

  • We share your details only with your consent, and only with partners relevant to your specific enquiry — we do not post your details to open marketplaces or unrelated mass mailing lists.
  • Once shared, each partner is an independent data controller that handles your information under its own privacy policy and terms. We are not responsible for a partner's data practices or for any service they provide.
  • We may receive a referral fee or commission from a partner. Depending on your jurisdiction (for example, under the California CCPA/CPRA), this value-based sharing may be treated as a "sale" or "sharing" of personal information, and you have the right to opt out.
  • You can withdraw your consent or ask us to stop sharing at any time using the contact details below (this won't affect sharing that already took place).

Cookies and analytics

The Site uses cookies and similar technologies, primarily for Google Analytics, to measure traffic and usage. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings, and you can opt out of Google Analytics using Google's opt-out browser add-on. Disabling cookies may affect some Site features but will not prevent you from reading our guides.

Email and the newsletter

Our newsletter is managed through a third-party email service provider (currently MailerLite), which stores your email address and engagement data on our behalf so we can deliver messages. Every email includes an unsubscribe link, and you can opt out at any time. We do not add your email to unrelated third-party mailing lists.

Affiliate links and partners

Some links on the Site are affiliate or referral links. If you click one and take an action (such as opening an account or contacting a recommended service), we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. Where you proceed to a partner's website or form, that partner's own privacy practices apply — we encourage you to review them.

Third parties we share data with

Beyond the partner professionals described under "Lead matching" above, we share limited data only with service providers that help us operate the Site, under their respective terms — including our analytics provider (Google Analytics), our email provider (MailerLite), and our hosting/content-delivery provider. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

Data retention

We keep newsletter and enquiry data for as long as needed to provide the service you requested, or until you ask us to delete it. Analytics data is retained according to our analytics provider's standard retention settings.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under laws such as the EU/UK GDPR or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your information, and to withdraw consent. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below. You can unsubscribe from emails at any time via the link in any message.

International visitors

The Site serves a primarily US audience but is open to buyers worldwide, and our service providers and partners may process data in the United States and other countries. By using the Site, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own.

Children's privacy

The Site is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be reflected on this page; your continued use of the Site after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact us

Questions about this policy, or want to withdraw consent or exercise a privacy right? Email us at privacy@migratestate.com.

This Privacy Policy is provided for general informational purposes and does not constitute legal advice.
